TPS 2

1. How to open a terminal:
   - Linux: Press Ctrl+Alt+T, or search for "Terminal" in applications
   - Mac: Press Cmd+Space and type "Terminal", or find it in Applications > Utilities
   - Windows: Press Win+R and type "cmd" or "powershell", or search for "Terminal"
